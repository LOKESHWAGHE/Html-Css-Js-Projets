DIGITAL ART FAIR

Digital Art Fair is the leading Web 3.0 annual art fair in the world of the digital art era. Showcasing some of the most innovative and exciting digital art and NFTs from around the globe. The fair provides a platform for emerging and established artists to exhibit their work, and for art enthusiasts and collectors to discover and acquire new digital artwork. ETHOS OF DIGITAL ART FAIR

As a pioneering art platform in a dynamic and rapidly evolving landscape of art, the Digital Art Fair firmly anchors itself upon the three cardinal ethos of sustainability, inclusivity, and equality.

EQUALITY

Using Digital Art & Culture as a medium to advocate for gender and LGBTQ equality

SUBSTAINABILITY

Promote the importance and urgency of protecting the planet and sustainability.

INCLUSIVITY

Providing education and opportunities for young artists, art practitioners and art collectors to learn and connect with Art & Tech.
